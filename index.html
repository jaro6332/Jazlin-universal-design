About Me

Hi! My name is Jazlin Rodriguez. I'm a first-generation college student and a CTD Major. During my first 2 years at CU, I was a computer science major but found that CTD catered to my needs and wants perfectly. I love my major and am so proud of the work I’ve done in it. I spend most of my time out of class working at Target and watching movies. I also love to watch baseball, especially in person at a game. Although I am a Colorado Rockies fan, I was given the amazing chance to watch game two of the World Series this year and had one of the best days of my life.

Professionally, I hope to be a UX designer or just be able to work anywhere in the tech industry. As of recently, I’m just very grateful and excited to be the first one in my family to graduate from an institution like CU. I’m very proud to be able to enjoy the things I do and look forward to the future. :)
